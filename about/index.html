Hi, I'm Yannick.

Based in Brighton, UK, I am a seasoned web developer with over 15 years of experience across various industries. I currently operate as a contractor through my company Yellow Raincoat Limited, offering backend consulting services to UK and overseas companies alike.

I have had the chance to live and work in cities like Paris, Montreal, and London, collaborating with a diverse range of company profiles including web agencies, startups, and product companies. I am fluent in both English and French and will happily work in either environment.

Beyond my consulting work, I actively contribute to the tech community through blogging and maintaining open-source projects. I also organise PHP Sussex, a Brighton-based meetup holding monthly events where local professionals come to network and listen to industry leaders.
